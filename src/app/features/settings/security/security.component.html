Two Factor Authentication (2FA)

Activate 2FA

1. You will need an authenticator mobile app to complete this process, such as one of the following:

  • Google Authenticator
  • Microsoft Authenticator
  • Duo Mobile

2. Scan the QR code with your authenticator

If you can't scan the code, you can either enter the secret key into your authenticator

**Note:** Make sure to store backup codes in a secure place in case you lose access to your primary authentication method. Click here to reveal the backup codes
